Vanderbilt Aerospace Design Lab (Rocket Team) · Mechanical Design, Integration & Testing

Mission Statement

The team will successfully launch a full-scale vehicle to achieve an apogee of 4200 feet above ground level (AGL), jettison the STEMCRaFT at 300 feet AGL, and safely land vehicle sections (excluding STEMCRaFT) utilizing the drogue and main parachutes to ensure all components will be ready to re-launch without any modifications or repairs. The STEMCRaFT will be a lander that jettisons from the fore rocket section and descends under parachute. At the surface the lander shall transmit flight telemetry and STEMnaut survivability metrics to the NASA ground station via RF over the 2M band.
